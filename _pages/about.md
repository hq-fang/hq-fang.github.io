Hi! I'm an undergraduate student and researcher at the University of Washington. I double major in Computer Science and Statistics, with a minor in Mathematics. I am fortunate to be co-advised by Professor [Ranjay Krishna](https://www.ranjaykrishna.com/) at the [RAIVN Lab](https://raivn.cs.washington.edu/) and Professor [Dieter Fox](https://homes.cs.washington.edu/~fox/) at the [Robotics and State Estimation Lab](https://rse-lab.cs.washington.edu/) in UW CSE, and advised by Professor [Jenq-Neng Hwang](https://people.ece.uw.edu/hwang/) at the [Information Processing Lab](https://ipl-uw.github.io/) in UW ECE. I am also pleased to collaborate with Dr [Jiafei Duan](https://duanjiafei.com/) and Dr [Ying Jin](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=MNrLDhwAAAAJ&hl=en). 

My research interests are broadly in the fields of computer vision, machine learning, and embodied AI. I am especially passionate about robot learning, imitation learning, behavior cloning, and foundation models for robotics.
